## Components of Power BI
- **Power BI Desktop** ‚Üí Development tool for creating reports and semantic models  
- **Power BI Service** ‚Üí Online platform for publishing, managing, and distributing reports  
- **Power BI Mobile** ‚Üí Mobile app for consuming reports in optimized layouts

## Flow of Power BI
1. Connect to data (Desktop)
2. Transform data with Power Query
3. Model relationships & calculations
4. Create visualizations and reports
5. Publish to Power BI Service
6. Distribute and manage reports/dashboards

---

## Building Blocks
- **Semantic Models** ‚Üí Data sources, transformations, relationships, calculations  
- **Visualizations** ‚Üí Charts, tables, KPIs, interactive visuals  

Reports = semantic model + visuals.  
Dashboards = pinned visuals from reports (high‚Äëlevel, single page).

---

## Dashboards
- Built in Power BI Service after publishing reports
- Consist of **tiles** pinned from visuals
- Provide high‚Äëlevel overview; tiles link back to detailed reports

---

## Power BI Service Features
- **Workspaces** ‚Üí Organize reports and dashboards; share with teams  
- **Apps** ‚Üí Bundle reports/dashboards for simplified distribution  
- **Template Apps** ‚Üí Prebuilt solutions (e.g., GitHub, Retail Analysis) that connect to your data  
- **Scheduled Refreshes** ‚Üí Keep semantic models up to date automatically
